Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the plan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dought
    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix toghether all the dought ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dought, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to corporare the last of the flour...
